Delivery and returns

Whem will my order be shipped?

We aim to dispatch your order within 1-3 days after receiving your order. Finnish and EU orders placed by 12PM are usually shipped the same day. Orders outside the EU placed by 10AM (CET) are usually shipped the same day. However, during busy times it might take up to 3 working days to process your order.

How long does the delivery take?

Shipping time depends on your location and the chosen delivery method. The delivery times given for different methods are estimated given by the carriers, and your order is usually delivered within the given shipping time estimate from the day you receive your order confirmation. However, the shipping times are estimates only, and sometimes delays may occur. We are not responsible for any delays once your order has been picked up from our warehouse by the carries.

Can I return an item?

Of course. We offer a 30 day return period, starting from the day you receive your order.

How do I return or exchange an item?

You will find the return form and return instructions in the envelope inside your package. You will find the return form, prepaid return-shipping label and return instructions in the envelope inside your package. Fill out the form, put it inside the package with the items you wish to return, and place the return-shipping label on the box over the prior delivery information. Drop off the package at your nearest PostNord pick up point or Posti (in Finland) or UPS pick-up point, or schedule a pick up with the UPS driver.
